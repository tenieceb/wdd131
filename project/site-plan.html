WDD131 - Project Site Plan

Site Name

Myspace of Old

- The website is a review of Myspace in the years of around 2006-2008

Site Purpose

This website will have a brief history of Myspace including how users learned to code to decorate their pages. It will have a small "try it" section that will enable users to manipulate a mock myspace page.

Scenarios

- Who used Myspace?

- How did Myspace users manipulate their pages?
