Who is Santa Claus?

Santa Claus has a long history and many names. He is known in many cultures around the world and usually associated with Christmas. He is known to travel to good children and give them gifts. There are various unpleasant "gifts" given for the naughty kids. He lives at the North Pole and has elves that help him make the toys.
