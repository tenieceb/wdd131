Writing to Santa

Little kids write to Santa Claus to tell him what they want for Christmas and if they were naughty or nice. Sometimes kids will even ask for Santa to gift their family certain items as well. The United States Postal Service has a program called USPS Operation Santa. With the letter addressed to Santa 123 Elf Road, North Pole, 88888. Since Santa is so busy, people can choose to become a Santa Helper and "Adopt a Letter". Adopting a letter means the Santa Helper will become a part of Santa's team and helping holiday wishes come true. For more information, click the link below!
